# Competitor Profile: OpsChain

## Positioning

OpsChain markets itself as the "enterprise-grade" workflow automation platform, with a strong focus on compliance, governance, and audit-readiness.

## Strengths

- SOC 2 Type II certified, with a full audit log included on all plans
- Granular, role-based permissions down to the field level
- Strong presence with regulated industries — finance, healthcare, logistics

## Weaknesses

- Implementation typically takes 6-8 weeks, often requiring a dedicated OpsChain solutions engineer
- Pricing is significantly higher than FlowPilot at comparable seat counts; prospects frequently describe it as "overkill" for mid-market needs
- UI is dated — multiple win/loss notes describe it as "clunky" compared to FlowPilot

## Where FlowPilot Loses to OpsChain

Audit log and field-level permissions are the most common reasons cited when prospects choose OpsChain over FlowPilot. See [[win-loss-notes]].

## Where FlowPilot Wins

Setup time and ease of use are consistently cited as reasons mid-market prospects choose FlowPilot over OpsChain, especially when compliance requirements are minimal.
