# Competitor Profile: Routely

## Positioning

Routely positions itself as the fast, low-cost workflow automation tool for small teams — "set up in an afternoon."

## Strengths

- Lowest price point in the category
- Same-day setup with minimal onboarding
- Simple, clean UI that smaller teams find approachable

## Weaknesses

- No audit log or activity history of any kind
- Permissions are all-or-nothing — admin vs. everyone, no role-based access
- No native integrations; all data movement is manual CSV export/import, the same limitation FlowPilot had before AutoSync

## Where FlowPilot Wins

Since AutoSync shipped, FlowPilot has a clear advantage over Routely for any prospect who has outgrown manual CSV workflows but isn't ready for OpsChain's price or complexity. See [[hearth-and-co]] for a recent example.

## Where FlowPilot Loses

Price-sensitive SMB prospects with very simple workflows sometimes choose Routely on cost alone, regardless of feature gaps.
