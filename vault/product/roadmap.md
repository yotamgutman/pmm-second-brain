# Product Roadmap — Snapshot

## Recently Shipped

- **AutoSync** (May 2026) — automated data sync for Salesforce, HubSpot, and NetSuite, replacing manual CSV export/import. See [[autosync-spec]].

## In Progress

- **AutoSync for Slack** — push sync status and conflict alerts into a customer's Slack workspace. Target: end of Q3 2026.
- **Audit Log (Enterprise)** — full activity log for compliance-focused customers. This has come up repeatedly in win/loss conversations (see [[win-loss-notes]]) and is a known gap versus [[opschain]].

## Under Consideration

- Expand AutoSync integrations to include Workday and SAP, based on inbound requests from Enterprise prospects.
- Self-serve sync conflict resolution UI — currently requires a support ticket.

## Notes

Audit Log has been deprioritized twice in favor of AutoSync and Bulk Approval Actions. Worth revisiting given how often it surfaces as a loss reason against [[opschain]], particularly as FlowPilot moves into larger accounts.
