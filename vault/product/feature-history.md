# Feature History — Last 4 Releases

## AutoSync — Shipped May 26, 2026

Automated data sync replacing manual CSV export/import for Salesforce, HubSpot, and NetSuite. See [[autosync-spec]].

## Bulk Approval Actions — Shipped March 10, 2026

Allows approvers to action multiple pending requests at once from the approvals queue. Reduced average approval time by roughly 30% in early adopter accounts.

## Custom Approval Routing — Shipped January 14, 2026

Lets admins define conditional routing rules — for example, requests over $5,000 route to Finance, all others route to the requester's direct manager.

## Mobile Approvals (iOS/Android) — Shipped November 2025

Push notifications and in-app approval actions for mobile users. Adoption has been steady but modest, around 18% of monthly active approvers.
